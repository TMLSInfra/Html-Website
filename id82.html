Health Insurance Masters is a national group of independent insurance agents dedicated to finding the best policy available for Individuals and Small Business Owners. We specialize in researching and offering innovative Health Insurance Solutions.  Every client is treated with respect and is given the individualized attention of our experienced agents.


We offer true Major Medical Health Insurance products, with a twist. There is an evolving trend in the mindset of our customers trying to survive today’s healthcare system.  They are looking for new, more cost effective ways to protect themselves. We embrace these changing times, by offering our customers access to a safety net.


For our Agents, Health Insurance Masters keeps abreast of changes in the healthcare system and health insurance industry.  These changes go hand in hand.  One always leads or follows the other.  We know we have to give our agents access to the most up to date, innovative, competitive products available.  Many agents are comfortable where they are in life, and in their careers, and find it difficult to learn new products and processes. That's ok. We provide all the tools and training needed to all new agents.

Health Insurance Masters is made up of client oriented, creative, energetic, motivated agents, and managers, determined to master and ride the changing new wave in health insurance with the most client oriented health insurance solutions available.
